What Is Ubisoft Plus?
=====================

Ubisoft Plus is a subscription service that provides access to a library of Ubisoft games through a Ubisoft Account. Depending on your subscription plan and supported platform, members can enjoy full game access, premium editions, day-one releases, and additional digital content.

Your Ubisoft Plus membership is linked to your Ubisoft Account, which stores your purchases, subscriptions, linked gaming platforms, saved settings, and account information.

===================================

Signing in to your Ubisoft Plus account allows you to:

* Access your Ubisoft Plus membership.
* Download eligible Ubisoft games.
* Play supported premium editions.
* Synchronize game progress.
* Manage your subscription.
* Access exclusive member benefits.
* Link supported gaming platforms.
* Manage account settings.
* View your game library.

Without signing in, you cannot access Ubisoft Plus membership features.

How to Ubisoft Plus Login
=========================

Signing in is quick and simple.

Open Ubisoft Connect or launch a supported Ubisoft game.

Select **Log In**.

Enter the email address associated with your Ubisoft Account.

Type your password.

Complete any required security verification.

Select **Log In**.

After successful authentication, your Ubisoft Plus membership and eligible games become available.

Ubisoft Plus Login on Windows PC
================================

Windows users can access Ubisoft Plus through Ubisoft Connect.

Open the Ubisoft Connect application.

Select **Log In**.

Enter your Ubisoft Account email address.

Provide your password.

Complete any verification request.

Once signed in, you can browse the Ubisoft Plus catalog, install games, and manage your subscription.

Ubisoft Plus Login on Xbox
==========================

If your subscription supports Xbox:

* Turn on your Xbox console.
* Sign in using your Microsoft account.
* Launch a supported Ubisoft title.
* Sign in to your Ubisoft Account if prompted.
* Link your Xbox account when requested.

After linking, your Ubisoft Plus membership will be available for supported games.

Ubisoft Plus Login on Cloud Gaming
==================================

Supported cloud gaming platforms may require your Ubisoft Account.

To sign in:

* Open the supported cloud gaming service.
* Launch an eligible Ubisoft title.
* Sign in with your Ubisoft Account.
* Complete any required verification.
* Begin playing supported games.

Your membership benefits are applied after successful authentication.

How to Reset Your Ubisoft Plus Password
=======================================

If you forget your password, resetting it is straightforward.

Visit the Ubisoft Account sign-in page.

Select **Forgot Password**.

Enter your registered email address.

Open the password reset email.

Follow the instructions to create a new password.

Return to the login page and sign in using your updated credentials.

Common Ubisoft Plus Login Problems
==================================

Some users may occasionally experience login issues.

Common causes include:

* Incorrect email address.
* Incorrect password.
* Forgotten account credentials.
* Internet connection problems.
* Ubisoft server maintenance.
* Two-step verification issues.
* Outdated Ubisoft Connect software.
* Account linking problems.

Most login issues can be resolved with simple troubleshooting.

How to Fix Ubisoft Plus Login Not Working
=========================================

If you cannot sign in, try these solutions:

* Verify your email address.
* Carefully enter your password.
* Reset your password if needed.
* Restart Ubisoft Connect.
* Restart your computer or console.
* Check your internet connection.
* Update Ubisoft Connect.
* Complete any required identity verification.
* Wait if Ubisoft services are temporarily unavailable.

These steps solve most Ubisoft Plus login issues.
